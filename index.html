    About Me

    Sudha M R

    I am a graduate student at the Entertainment Technology Center at the Carnegie Mellon University. My undergraduate degree is in Computer Science and Engineering from SSN College of Engineering, India. My work involves app development, games, animation, graphic design and virtual reality apps. I am a hobbyist artist who does pencil sketching, acrylic painting and oil painting. I am working towards a career in building products that involve graphics and emerging technologies. Above all, I am interested in interdisciplinary works that experiments with novel media applications.
